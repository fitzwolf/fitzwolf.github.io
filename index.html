About the Data

We are using Apple stocks since the crash of 2008. The aim is to show different ways people could have invested starting with $100,000. We compare simple long-term investment since the day of the crash, strategic buy-sell at key points, and as a comparison, investments in SPY (an ETF that tracks the S&P 500) and SPYI (a dividend ETF based on the S&P500).
