About This Project

Traditional wisdom has suggested that precious metals such as silver and gold are a good recession resistant investment. But they often do not produce as much gain during times of prosperity. So a question of interest is whether it is worth investing in silver and gold to protect for recessions are if there are other options that might also work. To answer this I have looked at how well an investment in silver as compared to other blue chip stocks (apple and microsoft) as well as an etf that tracks the S&P 500 closely to see what impact they have on your overall gains. To look specifically at recession impact the data used will start from the beginning of the Great Recession.
